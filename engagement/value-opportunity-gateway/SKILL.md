# Value Opportunity Gateway

A strategic lens for any consulting, advisory, or services organization. Analyzes every engagement, customer context, and deliverable through your value proposition — surfacing the most relevant offerings, framing the right narrative, and identifying where you can expand your relationship and impact with each client or prospect.

## Step 3: Identify Cross-Sell & Up-Sell Opportunities

After mapping to pillars, explicitly identify:

### Immediate Expansion Opportunities
What can be scoped NOW as an add-on or next step to the current engagement?
- A research engagement → natural bridge to implementation or advisory
- A one-time deliverable → convert to a retainer or ongoing subscription
- An event/workshop → deepen into a follow-on project

### Latent / Unstated Needs
What has the customer mentioned (or implied) that you could address but hasn't been formally scoped?
- Mentions of internal confusion about a topic → Education & Enablement
- Frustration with a vendor or partner → Competitive Assessment or channel support
- Mention of AI or data initiatives → Strategy or governance support
- Need to present to a board → Executive briefing or synthesized report

### Strategic Relationship Deepening
How can you move from transactional to embedded partner?
- Map which pillars the client has engaged with
- Identify whitespace across your portfolio
- Recommend 2–3 expansion conversations to initiate

## Step 5: Output Guidance

### For Discovery / Sales Conversations
- Lead with customer pain diagnosis
- Identify 1–2 primary entry offerings
- Surface 2–3 natural expansion paths
- Note which differentiators are most resonant for this customer

### For Deliverable Review / QA
- Flag where your POV and analyst voice are visible vs. generic summary
- Identify where bespoke tailoring to the client's context is evident
- Note where cross-sell language or "what's next" framing could be naturally inserted

### For Proposals or Scoping
- Lead with the business problem, not the service
- Recommend a primary offering + 1–2 adjacent services
- Frame pricing/scope in terms of outcomes, not hours
- Include a "what this unlocks" section that seeds future expansion

### For Strategic Account Reviews
- Assess which pillars the client has engaged with
- Map whitespace across your portfolio
- Recommend 2–3 expansion conversations to initiate
