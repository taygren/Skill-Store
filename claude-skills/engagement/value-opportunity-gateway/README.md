# value-opportunity-gateway

**Category:** Engagement  
**Trigger:** Any client engagement, proposal, deliverable review, or sales interaction where surfacing expansion opportunities matters

---

## What It Does

A strategic overlay skill for consulting and advisory organizations. Classifies the customer archetype and engagement stage, maps their stated and unstated needs to your service portfolio, identifies immediate cross-sell and up-sell opportunities, and frames the right narrative for the engagement. Applicable at any stage — discovery, delivery, QA, or account review.

This is a **configurable framework skill**. You populate your organization's specific pillars, archetypes, differentiators, and narrative language. The workflow and logic are universal; the specifics are yours.

---

## When to Use

- After gathering client context but before finalizing a proposal
- During deliverable review to ensure your POV and "what's next" framing is visible
- In account reviews to map whitespace across your portfolio
- Any time you want a structured lens on expansion opportunities

---

## Trigger Phrases

- "What should we be offering this client?"
- "Surface expansion opportunities for [account]"
- "Apply the value lens to this deliverable"
- "What's the cross-sell here?"
- "Frame our narrative for [prospect]"

---

## Setup Required

Before first use, customize the `SKILL.md` file:
1. Replace all `[Customize: Your Pillar N]` sections with your actual service pillars
2. Populate the archetype table with your customer types and entry points
3. Fill in your core differentiators with your firm's specific language
4. Update the narrative framing sentences for each archetype

---

## Key Inputs

| Input | Required? | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Customer / company context | ✅ | Industry, situation, known priorities |
| Engagement stage | ✅ | Discovery / Delivery / QA / Renewal |
| Deliverable or proposal (if reviewing) | Optional | Enables QA-mode framing |

---

## Outputs

Depending on context:
- **Discovery:** Pain diagnosis + 1–2 primary entry offerings + 2–3 expansion paths + resonant differentiators
- **Deliverable review:** POV visibility check + cross-sell language opportunities
- **Proposal scoping:** Problem-first framing + primary + adjacent offerings + "what this unlocks" section
- **Account review:** Pillar engagement map + whitespace + 2–3 expansion conversations to initiate
